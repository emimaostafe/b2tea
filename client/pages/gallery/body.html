Firefox & Pixel

Say hello to FireFox and Pixel, the charming cats who have made our tea shop their home. FireFox, with her vibrant orange coat and wild energy, is always ready to play and keep everyone on their toes. While FireFox is always ready for action (just like an orange cat), Pixel is a more laid-back cat who enjoys spending his days napping and lounging around the shop. Together, these two furry friends bring joy and warmth to our tea-loving community, and we couldn't imagine our shop without them.
